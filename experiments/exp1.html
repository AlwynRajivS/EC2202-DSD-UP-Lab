Logic Gates & Boolean Algebra — Verification Lab

Verify the basic and universal logic gates and the fundamental Boolean algebra theorems. Every gate and every law below is a live simulator — flip the switches and watch the IEEE/ANSI symbol, IC pinout and circuit diagram respond in real time.

1a. Basic Gates

AND, OR and NOT are the fundamental building blocks of digital logic. Toggle the inputs on each simulator to verify the truth table live.

1b. Universal & XOR Gates

NAND and NOR are universal gates — either one alone can implement any Boolean function. XOR is the "difference" gate, true only when inputs disagree.

1c. Boolean Algebra Theorems

Each law below draws its own L.H.S and R.H.S circuit from the expression. Flip the input switches — the LHS and RHS lamps always agree, proving the identity for every combination. Expand the table for the full reference truth table.
